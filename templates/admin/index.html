What is marketbot

Marketbot is a platform that allows us to automate many of the processes that we have today in our company, such as: WhatsApp messages, Google campaign automation, blog posts with keywords and good SEO and new customer acquisition.

WhatsApp Bot

Right now the automation of messages through WhatsApp is available, you can configure it in the settings area in the menu above.

This allows us to enter a URL and the code will read all the information on the page, then based on that, any user who asks a question on WhatsApp will be provided with information about your company or products on your page.
